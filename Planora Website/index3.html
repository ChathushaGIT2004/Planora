Why We Built This App

After experiencing the chaos of planning our own wedding and seeing friends struggle with the same challenges, we knew there had to be a better way. The endless spreadsheets, missed communications between vendors, and budget surprises were all too common.

We created Wedding Planner Pro to bring all the tools, resources, and professionals together in one beautiful, easy-to-use platform. Our mission is to make wedding planning joyful, organized, and stress-free for every couple.

Today, we've helped over 10,000 couples plan their dream weddings while maintaining their sanity (and budgets)!

Have Questions? We've Got Answers

Is the app free to use? +

The basic version is free forever with essential planning tools. Our premium version ($9.99/month) unlocks advanced features like vendor discounts and dedicated planner support.

Can I plan more than one event? +

Absolutely! Many users plan their wedding, engagement party, and rehearsal dinner all in one place. You can create separate projects for each event.

How do I contact a wedding planner? +

Our marketplace connects you with vetted planners. You can browse profiles, read reviews, and message planners directly through the app.

Is my personal data and budget private? +

Yes! We use bank-level encryption and never share your details without permission. You control what information vendors can see.
